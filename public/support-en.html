Frequently asked questions

Why does the app ask for Microphone/Camera/Photos permissions?

  • Microphone: required to measure sound levels and record audio.
  • Camera (optional): required only when you use video recording features.
  • Photos (optional): required only when you save exported files to your photo library.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app → Settings → tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used to buy Pro.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, go to: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Where are my recordings and reports stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device. When you export or share files, the destination app/service you choose controls how it handles the data.
